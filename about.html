Welcome To FELICIA

Felicia Technologies is a dynamic and innovative IT company, dedicated to providing top-notch technology solutions to a diverse range of industries

Our team of highly skilled professionals is committed to delivering excellence in every project, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible service and outcomes.

At Felicia Technologies, we specialize in custom software development, system integration, and IT consulting. Our expertise spans across various sectors, including hospitality, financial services, high-tech, agriculture, and transportation. We understand the unique challenges that each industry presents, and we tailor our solutions to meet those challenges head-on.

Our web development services include:


  • Website Design: We create custom website designs using the latest design trends and best practices.

  • Front-End Development: We use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build responsive and user-friendly front-end components.

  • Development: We use a variety of programming languages and frameworks, such as Python, Ruby on Rails, and Node.js, to build robust and scalable back-end systems.

  • Content Management Systems: We provide support for popular content management systems, such as WordPress and Drupal.

  • E-commerce Development: We help our clients build and maintain online stores using platforms such as Shopify and Magento.
We offer full-stack iOS, Android, and web development services, including requirements analysis, quality assurance, and project management, to create a fully-fledged, stable, and scalable product for various platforms.

For mobile app development, we provide dynamic, user-centric applications, from concept to deployment, for iOS, Android, and Windows, using React Native, Flutter, and Apple Watch development. We also offer PWA development for cross-platform compatibility.

Our web development services focus on creating responsive, scalable, and engaging websites, from e-commerce platforms to custom solutions, using Angular.js, React.js, Node.js, Laravel, and Python. We also provide UI/UX design, data science and analytics, Power BI development, chatbot development, and emerging technology services.

We are committed to turning your ideas into impactful digital experiences and helping you grow your business into the future.

Business Planning

Our business planning services include:

Market Analysis: We conduct in-depth market research to identify trends, opportunities, and threats, and to understand the competitive landscape.

Financial Planning: We help our clients develop realistic financial projections, including revenue, expense, and cash flow forecasts.

Financial Analaysis

Our company provides comprehensive financial analysis services to help businesses make informed decisions. Our analysis includes a thorough examination of financial statements, such as balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements, to evaluate a company's financial performance over time. We use various financial analysis techniques, such as horizontal and vertical analysis, to identify trends and patterns in a company's financial data. We also calculate financial ratios to assess a company's profitability, liquidity, and operational efficiency.

legal Advisory

Our legal advisors possess excellent analytical, research, and writing skills, enabling them to structure solutions for complex issues and provide support in legal matters. They are also skilled at making informed judgments and working well within a team. Strong communication skills, the ability to manage a large workload with tight deadlines, and staying up-to-date with relevant laws are essential for a successful legal advisor in our company.

Our legal advisors come from diverse backgrounds and have taken various paths to their current roles. Regardless of their background, our legal advisors share a passion for law and a commitment to providing exceptional legal advisory services to our clients.
